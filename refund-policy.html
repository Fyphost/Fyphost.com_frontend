Refund Policy

Effective Date: 06/04/25

Home / Refund Policy

1. Overview

At Fyphost.com, we strive to provide high-quality web hosting services. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we offer a 30-day refund policy on eligible services.

2. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

Customers are eligible for a full refund if a cancellation request is submitted within 30 days of the initial purchase date. This guarantee applies only to first-time customers and select hosting services.

3. Non-Refundable Services

The following items are non-refundable:

  • Domain registrations, renewals, or transfer fees
  • SSL certificates after activation
  • Setup fees and administrative fees
  • Custom packages or add-ons once delivered

4. Requesting a Refund

To request a refund:

  • Log in to your Fyphost account
  • Submit a support ticket or contact support@fyphost.com
  • Include your account details and reason for cancellation

Refunds will be processed and credited to the original payment method within 7–10 business days.

5. Cancellation After 30 Days

If you cancel your service after 30 days, you will not be eligible for a refund. However, your services will remain active until the end of the billing cycle.

6. Abuse of Refund Policy

Fyphost reserves the right to deny refund requests from users who violate our Terms and Conditions or abuse the refund process (e.g., repeated cancellations and reactivations).
