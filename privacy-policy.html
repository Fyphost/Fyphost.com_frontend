1. Introduction

Fyphost.com ("Fyphost", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use our website and hosting services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect personal and technical information, including:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, and billing details
  • Login credentials and account data
  • IP address, browser type, device information, and usage logs
  • Cookies and tracking data

3. How We Use Information

The data we collect is used to:

  • Deliver and manage web hosting services
  • Provide customer support and service notifications
  • Process payments and prevent fraud
  • Improve and monitor our website and systems
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to track user behavior, improve site performance, and personalize content. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

5. Sharing of Data

We do not sell your data. We may share information with:

  • Trusted payment processors
  • Cloud infrastructure and analytics providers
  • Regulatory or law enforcement agencies if required

6. Data Security

We implement strong security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no system can guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy. Once data is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it securely.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal data
  • Withdraw consent or restrict processing
  • Object to direct marketing
  • Request a copy of your data (data portability)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@fyphost.com.

9. Emails and Marketing

We may send service-related and marketing emails. You can unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time using the link in the email.

10. Third-Party Services

We may link to third-party websites or services. Their privacy practices are not covered by this policy. Please review their policies before sharing personal information.

11. Children's Privacy

Fyphost does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted personal data, please contact us for removal.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be available on this page with the updated effective date.
